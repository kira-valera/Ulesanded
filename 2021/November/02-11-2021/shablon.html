about

The project developers, service developers, management leadership, investors group are the company shareholders.

A certain percentage of shares were set aside as an incentive for the most successful partners who are involved in the project promotion. The most successful partners, who have taken the most active part in the company life following the accounting period results, will be included in the list of joint owners.

INCONEX Ltd. specializes in developing and launching of new projects and organizing international conferences & exhibitions in Russia. Due to work with the wild net of Russian and international editions and associations, the exhibitions turn to international events. Exclusive database of the biggest industrial enterprises of Russia and CIS, and elaborated advertising company enable us to organize exhibitions with remarkably wide list of participants and visitors.

The aim of the company: to assure the business cooperation of specialists, to create favorable conditions for developing regional and international relations, to arrange contacts of suppliers with potential buyers, to introduce new technologies to specialists, to promote new products on the Russian market.

Exhibition Company INCONEX has been the member of Moscow Commerce and Industrial Chamber science 17.05.2005
